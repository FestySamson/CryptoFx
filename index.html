Ian Somerhalder Faces Child Support Crisis as Court Freezes Assets and Business Holdings

Los Angeles Superior Court $32,180 Arrears Date: 14th september 2025

Ian Somerhalder and his wife at a recent event

Actor Ian Somerhalder, widely known for his role in The Vampire Diaries, is facing one of the most difficult legal challenges of his career. The Los Angeles Superior Court has ordered him to repay $32,180 in overdue child support, triggering strict federal enforcement actions that have placed his properties and business ventures under court control.

Although the amount may seem relatively small compared to Hollywood standards, the court took swift action after Somerhalder delayed payments, invoking aggressive measures under 42 U.S.C. § 666, a federal statute that allows states to enforce child support through property liens, bank freezes, business seizures, and even contempt proceedings that can lead to jail time.
